# 🕯️ Minecraft Horror Mod

> *Darkness has never felt so real.*

A Fabric mod for Minecraft 1.21 that transforms your world into a terrifying survival experience. Face horrifying new creatures, explore cursed dimensions, discover dread-filled structures, and survive spine-chilling jumpscares — if you dare.

---

## 🎮 Features

### 👹 New Scary Mobs & Monsters
Encounter a host of terrifying new entities that stalk you in the dark. Each monster has unique AI behavior, making every encounter unpredictable and deadly.

### 😱 Jumpscares & Sounds
A dynamic horror audio system that reacts to your environment. Eerie ambient sounds, sudden jumpscares, and bone-chilling effects will keep you on edge at all times.

### 🌑 Horror Dimensions & Biomes
Step through the veil into dark new dimensions. Twisted biomes corrupt the landscape with an unsettling atmosphere unlike anything in vanilla Minecraft.

### 🏚️ Creepy Structures & Dungeons
Discover abandoned ruins, haunted mansions, and cursed dungeons scattered across the world — each filled with traps, lore, and terrifying rewards.

## 🔧 Installation

1. Download and install [Fabric Loader](https://fabricmc.net/use/) for Minecraft **1.21**.
2. Download the latest [Fabric API](https://modrinth.com/mod/fabric-api) and place it in your `mods` folder.
3. Download the latest release of **Minecraft Horror Mod** from the [Releases](../../releases) page.
4. Place the `.jar` file into your `.minecraft/mods` folder.
5. Launch Minecraft using the Fabric profile.
6. Try not to scream. 🕯️
